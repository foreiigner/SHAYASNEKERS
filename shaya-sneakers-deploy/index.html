Welcome to Shaya Sneakers & Clothes

At Shaya, we believe true luxury lies in curation—and every wardrobe deserves a touch of excellence. We offer an exclusive edit of brand‑new and meticulously selected preloved fashion, pairing modern sophistication with the charm of timeless pieces.

From rare sneakers to elevated streetwear and statement vintage finds, each item is hand‑picked for its quality, provenance, and design integrity. Our preloved collection isn't just recycled—it's revered, restored, and ready to elevate your look.

Founded and directed by Yahya Juma (Msizwa), Shaya embodies a visionary approach to fashion: where craftsmanship meets conscience, and every piece carries a story. You can explore his aesthetic vision and style inspiration over on Instagram: @msizwa23.

Experience curated elegance. Embrace responsible luxury. Welcome to Shaya.
